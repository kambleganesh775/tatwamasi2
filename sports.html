Aditi Institute of Sports

Nurturing Young Talent

Aditi Institute of Sports helps young people build real skill and real character, through structured coaching and honest hard work. We care as much about who a young athlete becomes as what they can do on the field. A confident player, a good teammate — that's the real win.

What We Train For

"We teach our students to work hard, respect the other team, back up their teammates, and understand that character is built through effort, not luck."

Aditi Institute of Sports | Cricket

Cricket Training (U-13 | U-15 | U-18)

Our cricket training builds real skill and game sense from an early age, with coaching matched to each age group — building strong basics and getting players ready for the next level.

  • Under 13 — Getting the Basics Right
  • Under 15 — Learning to Play Smart
  • Under 18 — Ready for the Next Level

Focus: Batting technique, handling spin & seam, field placement, match practice.

Aditi Institute of Sports | Football

Football Training (U-13 | U-15 | U-18)

Our football training builds agility, teamwork, and game sense — with structured sessions across age groups to build skill, stamina, and a real feel for the game.

  • Under 13 — Ball Control Basics
  • Under 15 — Learning Team Play
  • Under 18 — Match-Ready Stamina

Focus: Pace, passing, creating space, teamwork, fitness.

Aditi Institute of Sports | Wellness

Yoga & Fitness Training

Our yoga and fitness sessions build strength, flexibility, and focus — blending old practices with modern training to help students stay fit, avoid injury, and feel balanced in body and mind.

Focus: Mindfulness, breathing (Pranayama), core strength, recovery.
