Sports: Discipline, Teamwork, Excellence

Through Aditi Institute of Sports, an initiative by Tatwamasi Foundation, we develop young athletes into confident performers and committed team players. We nurture physical skill alongside character, grit, and leadership.

Aditi Institute of Sports | Cricket

Cricket Training Academy

Cricket is more than a sport in India; it's a channel for community, passion, and opportunity. Our cricket program focuses on perfecting the core mechanics: batting footwork, bowling spin/speed, and game strategy from an early age.

With structured U-13, U-15, and U-18 coaching paths, athletes prepare for competitive district, state, and club tournaments, opening pathways to professional sports careers.

Aditi Institute of Sports | Football

Football Coaching Program

Agility, rapid strategic positioning, and physical endurance guide our football program. Under the supervision of certified coaches, young boys and girls learn modern tactical football concepts, pressing formations, and quick transitions on our professional 90m pitch in rural India.

We believe in building confident team players who carry the grit and responsibility of ensemble play on and off the field.

Aditi Institute of Sports | Wellness

Yoga & Physical Fitness

A balanced athlete requires flexibility, structural recovery, and mental concentration. We blend traditional Hatha and Vinyasa yoga breathing blocks with modern plyometric conditioning classes.

This program is open to both our academy athletes (for post-match muscle recovery and injury prevention) and the general community (to encourage balanced physical and mental health).
