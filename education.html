Education

Building Minds, Shaping Futures

From first lessons to real excellence. Education is where everything else starts. At Tatwamasi Foundation, we believe learning should wake something up in a child — not just fill their notebook.

How We Teach

We run two institutes that work together, each doing its part in helping the young people of Jiaganj go further: Aditi Vidya Niketan School (opening 2027) and Aditi Institute of Excellence (running since 2023). Together, they hold a child at every stage — one building the base, the other opening doors to what comes next.

FOUNDATION EDUCATION · COMING 2027

Aditi Vidyaniketan

A New-Age CBSE School

Equity & Access — 75–80% of our students come from Economically Weaker Sections — good education here isn't decided by income.

Key Highlights

  • Learning by doing, not just by listening
  • Every child celebrated — on good days and hard ones
  • Sports, arts, and music built into the school day
  • Set in Jiaganj, Murshidabad
  • First school of its kind in the district
  • Built to be copied — in other towns like ours, across India
AFTER-SCHOOL SUPPORT · SINCE 2023

Aditi Institute of Excellence — After-School Support

Helping young minds grow, in a place that actually supports them. Running since 2023 in Jiaganj, Murshidabad, focused on:

  • Grades 1–9, Core Subjects — English, Mathematics, Spoken English
  • Digital Classrooms — Real computer access to build learning and digital skills
  • Affordable Fees — Kept low on purpose, with support for families who need it
