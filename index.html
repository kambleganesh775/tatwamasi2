The Mission

Rooted in India,
Reaching Every Soul

Tatwamasi Foundation works at the intersection of rural India's most urgent needs - education, healthcare, sports, environment and music. We reach the children and communities who have been left furthest behind, and walk alongside them as they discover what they are truly capable of.

"We believe that true empowerment is not given - it is awakened. Every village holds wisdom. Every child carries potential. Our work is simply to create the conditions where both can flourish."

Our programmes are built for scale, but delivered with care. We partner with local leaders, respect the community traditions, and measure success not in numbers, but in lives transformed and futures rewritten.

Our Story

A Legacy of Awakening Potential

Founded in 2022 by visionaries including Arijit Singh in rural India, we work daily to build infrastructure that guarantees a future of quality and dignity.

Scroll horizontally
2022

Foundation Established

Tatwamasi Foundation is launched with a clear, bold vision to serve communities and unleash youth potential in rural India.

2023

Three flagship Institutes Launched

Aditi Institute of Excellence (Education), Aditi Institute of Sports, and Aditi SurSadhanalaya (Music & Arts) go live, welcoming their first learners.

2024-26

Community Growth & Drives

Expansion of athletic coaching, arts training, environment beekeeping support, and medical drives, providing healthcare relief to 100+ families annually.

2027

School & Hospital Opening

The upcoming CBSE model school Aditi Vidyaniketan and our integrated regional healthcare and emergency facility open their doors to the region.
