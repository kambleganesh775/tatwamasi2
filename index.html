THE MISSION

Rooted in India,
Reaching Every Home

Tatwamasi Foundation works where the need is greatest — education, health, sports, music and the environment — in small towns like ours that don't yet have easy access to good schools or good doctors. We stay with the children and families who are usually the last to get noticed, and walk with them as they find out what they can do.

"Empowerment isn't something we hand over — it's something we wake up. Every community already holds wisdom. Every child already carries potential. Our work is just to make the space where both can grow."

We build our work to grow, but we deliver it with care. We work with local people, respect the way things already work here, and measure success not just in numbers, but in the lives that actually change.

Our Story

Building since 2022

Founded in 2022 by visionaries including Arijit Singh, we work daily to build infrastructure that guarantees a future of quality and dignity.

2022

We Began

Tatwamasi Foundation started with one goal: serve the community in Murshidabad.

2023

Three Institutes Open

Aditi Institute of Excellence, Aditi Institute of Sports, and Aditi Sursadhanaloy all began.

2024–26

Growing Together

Reaching more families, going deeper with each one — alongside health support for 100+ patients a year.

2027

School & Hospital

Aditi Vidyaniketan school and an integrated health facility to start in Jiaganj, Murshidabad

Next Few Years

Sports Facility

We will start a state-of-the-art sports facility

Years Ahead

Replication

We replicate the Jianganj model in other resource constrained parts of India

Our Chapters

Five parts of the same work

Everything we do fits into five parts — education, sports, music, health, and care for the land. Each one is built with the community, tested as we go, and shaped by what actually helps.

Education

Quality learning for every child, from foundational literacy to digital skills - so that no young mind is left behind by circumstance of birth.

Learn More

Sports

Harnessing the power of sport to build discipline, teamwork, and confidence unlocking pathways to new opportunities.

Learn More

Music

Preserving India's rich folk traditions while nurturing new creative voices music as healing, identity, and a bridge across generations.

Learn More

Healthcare

Preventive care, maternal health, and nutrition programmes that protect entire families and strengthen the foundation of community wellbeing.

Learn More

Environment

Protecting the land that sustains local livelihoods through tree planting, water conservation, and sustainable agricultural practices.
