Healthcare

Caring for Health, Caring for Tomorrow

No child should suffer from an illness that's easy to treat. No family should have to travel far for basic care. Tatwamasi Foundation runs regular medical camps in small towns like ours where good healthcare is still out of reach, teaches preventive health, and is building an integrated health facility for the years ahead. We work with real doctors who understand and respect the community they're serving.

What We Value

Our healthcare programs are guided by three simple values: treating patients close to home, teaching them how to prevent diseases before they happen, and ensuring that no one is turned away because they can't pay.

Medical Camps

Caring for our community, one camp at a time

We run regular medical camps in rural areas where healthcare is hard to find — doing full check-ups, teaching preventive health, and giving people the care they need close to where they live.

We work with real doctors who understand the community and respect their way of life.

Our Patient Support Fund helps patients pay for surgeries, specialized medicine, and treatments they cannot afford.

Support Fund Statistics

We support an average of 100+ patients every year with critical surgical funding, medicine, and wellness kits.

Integrated Health Facility

Quality care, close to home

We are building a center that makes it easy to get treated in Jiaganj, Murshidabad — with clean wards, proper equipment, and doctors who listen.

To ensure long-term sustainability and build local capacities, the facility will include a specialized education program training rural youth in nursing, diagnostics, and paramedical care, opening up medical careers within the district.

  • General Outpatient Care — Daily clinics for common health issues
  • Mother & Child Health — Dedicated maternity care and pediatric support
  • Basic Wards — Clean, comfortable beds for inpatient recovery
  • Medical Education Wing — Training local youth to become healthcare workers
